How Does This Work?

1. Create a Platformer Game using Godot

Godot is a free game engine you can use to create games! Use the guides and join the workshops in Slack to create your 2D platformer. Also, learn to setup a Github repo for your project.

2. Make Your Game Unique

Create a backstory or choose a theme or style for your platformer. Then, keep adding on to your game and adding new features to make it better!

3. Publish your Project to Itch.io

Itch.io is a website for people to share and play video games. Make an account, export your game, and publish it.

4. Ship your Project and Share it

Share your amazing project in the #ship channel and the #athena-award channel. You can share the Itch.io link for your game with family, friends, and anyone else.

5. Earn Prizes! CONGRATS!

You will get stickers, Artifacts and other prizes
